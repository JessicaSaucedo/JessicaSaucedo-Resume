Jessica Saucedo

Pharmacy Technician

Jessica Saucedo is a 4th-year undergrad from the University of California, Riverside. She will graduate in a couple of weeks with a Bachelor of Science in Biology.

Jessica has done lots of paid and volunteer work during her time as a UCR undergrad. Since her sophomore year, she has volunteered over 500 hours in Dr. Anderson's Ecology Lab. She has been involved in various projects regarding protist density, abundance, and living situations. One of the projects that she was involved in won 2 prestigious UC awards. She has also been paid to work part-time in the Anderson Lab over the summer through scholarships. Jessica worked in UCR's Academic Advising Center as an Organic Chemistry supplemental instructor where she would make up plans and assist students in the various Organic Chemistry classes. She also hosts study groups for her harder division classes for any of the students that care to attend, in person and over Zoom.

She also currently works at CVS as a pharmacy technician since 2020. The pharmacist she works under is connected to the UC San Diego pharmacy school and is very certain Jessica is a great candidate. Her dream is to attend the Skaggs School of Pharmacy and Pharmaceutical Sciences at UC San Diego after working in the lab to get experience in the pharmacy world. She also uses her free time to go volunteer at a non-profit animal shelter where she cares for small animals and even fosters cats or dogs if they need a temporary home.
